About Me

I am a programmer born in Canada and have moved all over Alberta and British Columbia. I graduated from the university of British Columbia with my computer science bachelors degree in the summer of 2017. From there I have worked remotely for a small dedicated startup and a large multinational company and have learned much from them, such as various industry standards and practices, and more specifically full stack web development and mobile development for Android and iOS.
I find great satisfaction in creating a piece of intuitive interactive media, with respect to both its visual design and it’s background logic. Presenting such projects after putting in the effort to make it as robust, efficient, and visually pleasing as possible means a great deal to me. When I am not working, learning on my own time or working on personal projects, I love to go on hikes with my dog, hang out with my friends or settle down with a nice book.
